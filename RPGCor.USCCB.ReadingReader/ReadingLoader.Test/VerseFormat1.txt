              <p>Lectionary: 398</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  
            
    <div class="wr-block b-verse bg-white padding-bottom-m">
    <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
        <div class="p-wrap col-lg-10 offset-lg-1 col-xl-8 offset-xl-2 col-xxl-6 offset-xxl-3 ">
          <div class="innerblock">
                          <div class="content-header">
                <h3 class="name">Reading 1&nbsp;</h3>
                <div class="address">
                                                                                                                                      <a href="https://bible.usccb.org/bible/jeremiah/2?1 ">JER 2:1-3, 7-8, 12-13 </a>                  
                </div>
              </div>
              <div class="content-body">
                <p>This word of the LORD came to me:<br>
Go, cry out this message for Jerusalem to hear!<br>
I remember the devotion of your youth,<br>
how you loved me as a bride,<br>
Following me in the desert,<br>
in a land unsown.<br>
Sacred to the LORD was Israel,<br>
the first fruits of his harvest;<br>
Should any presume to partake of them,<br>
evil would befall them, says the LORD.</p>

<p>When I brought you into the garden land&nbsp;<br>
to eat its goodly fruits,<br>
You entered and defiled my land,<br>
you made my heritage loathsome.<br>
The priests asked not,<br>
“Where is the LORD?”<br>
Those who dealt with the law knew me not:<br>
the shepherds rebelled against me.<br>
The prophets prophesied by Baal,<br>
and went after useless idols.</p>

<p>Be amazed at this, O heavens,<br>
and shudder with sheer horror, says the LORD.<br>
Two evils have my people done:<br>
they have forsaken me, the source of living waters;<br>
They have dug themselves cisterns,<br>
broken cisterns, that hold no water.</p>
              </div>
                      </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="wr-block b-verse bg-white padding-bottom-m">
    <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
        <div class="p-wrap col-lg-10 offset-lg-1 col-xl-8 offset-xl-2 col-xxl-6 offset-xxl-3 ">
          <div class="innerblock">
                          <div class="content-header">
                <h3 class="name">Responsorial Psalm </h3>
                <div class="address">
                                                                                                                                      <a href="https://bible.usccb.org/bible/psalms/36?6 ">PS&nbsp;36:6-7AB, 8-9, 10-11</a>                  
                </div>
              </div>
              <div class="content-body">
                R. (10a) <strong>With you is the fountain of life, O Lord.</strong><br>O LORD, your mercy reaches to heaven;<br>your faithfulness, to the clouds.<br>Your justice is like the mountains of God;<br>your judgments, like the mighty deep.<br>R. <strong>With you is the fountain of life, O Lord.</strong><br>How precious is your mercy, O God!<br>The children of men take refuge in the shadow of your wings.<br>They have their fill of the prime gifts of your house;<br>from your delightful stream you give them to drink.<br>R. <strong>With you is the fountain of life, O Lord.</strong><br>For with you is the fountain of life,<br>and in your light we see light.<br>Keep up your mercy toward your friends,<br>your just defense of the upright of heart.<br>R. <strong>With you is the fountain of life, O Lord.</strong>
              </div>
                      </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="wr-block b-verse bg-white padding-bottom-m">
    <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
        <div class="p-wrap col-lg-10 offset-lg-1 col-xl-8 offset-xl-2 col-xxl-6 offset-xxl-3 ">
          <div class="innerblock">
                          <div class="content-header">
                <h3 class="name">Alleluia&nbsp;</h3>
                <div class="address">
                                                                                                                                      <a href="https://bible.usccb.org/bible/matthew/11?25 ">MT 11:25</a>                  
                </div>
              </div>
              <div class="content-body">
                R. <strong>Alleluia, alleluia.</strong><br>Blessed are you, Father, Lord of heaven and earth;<br>you have revealed to little ones the mysteries of the Kingdom.<br>R. <strong>Alleluia, alleluia.&nbsp;</strong>
              </div>
                      </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="wr-block b-verse bg-white padding-bottom-m">
    <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
        <div class="p-wrap col-lg-10 offset-lg-1 col-xl-8 offset-xl-2 col-xxl-6 offset-xxl-3 ">
          <div class="innerblock">
                          <div class="content-header">
                <h3 class="name">Gospel&nbsp;</h3>
                <div class="address">
                                                                                                                                      <a href="https://bible.usccb.org/bible/matthew/13?10 ">MT 13:10-17 </a>                  
                </div>
              </div>
              <div class="content-body">
                <p>The disciples approached Jesus and said,<br>
“Why do you speak to the crowd in parables?”<br>
He said to them in reply,<br>
“Because knowledge of the mysteries of the Kingdom of heaven<br>
has been granted to you, but to them it has not been granted.<br>
To anyone who has, more will be given and he will grow rich;<br>
from anyone who has not, even what he has will be taken away.<br>
This is why I speak to them in parables, because<br>
<em>they look but do not see and hear but do not listen or understand.</em><br>
Isaiah’s prophecy is fulfilled in them, which says:</p>

<p><em>You shall indeed hear but not understand,<br>
you shall indeed look but never see.<br>
Gross is the heart of this people,<br>
they will hardly hear with their ears,<br>
they have closed their eyes,<br>
lest they see with their eyes<br>
and hear with their ears<br>
and understand with their hearts and be converted<br>
and I heal them.</em></p>

<p>“But blessed are your eyes, because they see,<br>
and your ears, because they hear.<br>
Amen, I say to you, many prophets and righteous people<br>
longed to see what you see but did not see it,<br>
and to hear what you hear but did not hear it.”<br>
<br>
&nbsp;</p>
              </div>
                      </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
